# CAN IDs

yes i understand that different device classes can share a can id but i like it this way

## Misc (`00`-`09`)

**RoboRIO:** `00`

**REV PDH:** `01`

**Pigeon2:** `09`

## Drivetrain (`10`-`19`)

**Front Left Drive:** `10`

**Front Left Turn:** `11`

**Back Left Drive:** `12`

**Back Left Turn:** `13`

**Front Right Drive:** `14`

**Front Right Turn:** `15`

**Back Right Drive:** `16`

**Back Right Turn:** `17`

## Intake (`20`-`29`)

**Intake & First Star Wheel Motor:** `20`

**Indexer/Second Star Wheel Motor:** `22`

**Belt Motor:** `23`

**Over Bumper Intake Spin:** `25`

**Over Bumper Intake Pivot:** `26`

## Shooter (`30`-`39`)

**Tilt Servo:** `PWM 0`

**Pan Motor:** `31`

**Flywheel Motor 1:** `32`

**Flywheel Motor 2:** `33`
